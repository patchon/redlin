Inventorying the infrastructure for consistency

This project was created to get a good overview of the infrastructure “as a whole”. This meant querying every part of the infrastructure and then summorize it in a nice manner (later on this was presented in a web gui).

Keeping your infrastructure in order and standardized is essential for automation and expansion. With this sort of tooling you (and other stakeholders) can get a good overview of your infrastructure.
